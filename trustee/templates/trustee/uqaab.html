ABOUT UQAAB EDUCATIONAL & CHARITABLE TRUST (Regd No. 93 -2011-12)

UQAAB CHARITABLE & EDUCATIONAL Trust is a non-profit, non-governmental, educational and charitable organization which was initiated and set up in the year 2011. The Trust has been registered under Indian Trusts Act Vide Registration Number 93/11 and has been functioning in Bangalore in India.

UQAAB CHARITABLE & EDUCATIONAL Trust is working for the upliftment of the poorest of poor and serves all communities irrespective of caste, creed and religion.

The activities include providing quality education by encouragement of learning, basic Computer Education, offering school uniforms, text and note books, school bags etc. to encourage education and tutoring services to children of economically weaker section of the society.

  1. Basically established with objective to establish, conduct and encourage educational institutions of any level to impart quality education, training and discipline among youth in different fields such as education, health, cultural, socio-economic and others irrespective of caste, creed and religion.
  2. To promote socioeconomic welfare activities particularly amond women, children and other weaker section of the society
  3. To organize and conduct training courses and camps for the betterment of unemployed men and women
  4. To organize and conduct schools or welfare programmes for women and spastic children
  5. To work for rural development and environment awareness and protection
  6. To public educational, literacy and spiritual works

We have provided primary, secondary and pre-university education to underprivileged children who are now successfully accomplished in life.
